Abhyanga (60/90 minutes)

Tajview, Agra - IHCL SeleQtions

Abhyanga, done by two therapists allowing the entire body to apply oil at the same time, is a full-body and synchronized continuous oil massage. The massage oil used in this therapy is specifically selected according to your dosha (body type) and is locally prepared. The relaxing rhythmic strokes simultaneously increase blood circulation and remove toxins thus owning deep relaxation. In this way, Abhyanga treatment has a significant effect on the body which is reflected in the psycho-affective aspect as well.

Benefits: Alters the texture and tone of the dermal covering, thus, performs the following functions: Skin conditioning, free blood flow during oxygen movement in the body, Detoxicific treatment, and the mind may drift into a profound state of relaxation.

Shirodhara (45 minutes)

Tajview, Agra - IHCL SeleQtions

Shirodhara is one of the most popular Ayurvedic practices that uses a constant flow of warm herbal oil gently dropped on the forehead (the third eye). This is deeply meditative for soothing the mind, and it helps to increase concentration, reduces stress and anxiety. Shirodhara is a perfect Ayurvedic therapy to get mental clarity and a relaxation effect.

Benefits: As a result of which insomnia is gone to some extent, stress is more alleviated, concentration is increased and headaches as well as migraines are not that painful.

Vishrama (90 minutes)

Tajview, Agra - IHCL SeleQtions

Vishrama Full Body Massage is a deep and spiritual massage that clears all the stress in the whole body by using light and long strokes. The massage oil is mixed with some relaxing herbs in this treatment to be fully stress-free and to feel restful.

Benefits: Relaxes muscles, increases blood flow as well as decreases stress.
